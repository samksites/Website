            About

            A recent graduate of James Madison University, I found a passion for coding and data in college. I enjoy the challenge of a complex coding project, which is like a puzzle to be solved. The process can be long and sometimes frustrating and requires commitment and determination, but the experience of getting to the end and seeing the project succeed is so rewarding.

            Outside of my academic pursuits, I have been a lifelong competitive swimmer. Like coding, swimming demands commitment, perseverance, and hard work to reach your goals. I was a 14-year swimmer for my summer-league team as well as a coach for four years, began year-round swimming with a club team at 11, was a four-year varsity swimmer in high school, and swam for the JMU club team. My favorite event is the 50 freestyle.

            My goal is to pursue a career in software engineering and data science, to work within a community of incredible coders, and to continue to extend my knowledge and deepen my learning in this field.
